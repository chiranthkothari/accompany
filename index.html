Sakha Sakhi Dharma

Our Guiding Compass

Five tenets that guide every action — because trust takes time, and it isn't built on apps.

①

Upasthiti

Presence

We're with them, not on a phone or with a bigger group. When Amma needs a hand on a temple step, a Sakhi's hand is already hovering.

②

Dhairya

Patience

We match their pace. One temple is enough if that's their pace. We don't “cover” yatras — we live them.

③

Maryada

Dignity

Zero tolerance for inappropriate conduct. Absolute financial boundaries. What happens on yatra, stays on yatra.

④

Suraksha

Safety

The #1 priority. We pre-walk every route and sleep next door during travel. At 2 AM, a human answers — not voicemail.

⑤

Sambandh

Relationship

We call your parent by name. We remember the filter coffee, no sugar. We send the one photo that matters: them, smiling.

Family Accompany Questions

Before you ask, we thought of it

01What exactly is Accompany?
Accompany provides trusted, background-verified Sakhas & Sakhis for senior citizens. We're not a cab service or medical caregivers. We're the friends who accompany you during travel, for doctor visits, temples, the beach, kutcheris, morning walks, shopping — or just for coffee. We stay, engage, and ensure you're safe and comfortable.
02What is a Sakha / Sakhi?
Not a volunteer. Not a caregiver. A companion. In our tradition a Sakha is Krishna to Arjuna — not a servant, but a friend who walks with you and stays even when the path is hard. A Sakhi is the friend who knows you need water before you ask. Each one is guided by our five tenets: Presence, Patience, Dignity, Safety, and Relationship.
03Which cities do you operate in?
We're starting from Namma Chennai with pan-city coverage. If you love this thought and like our services, spread the word — we will eventually cater to other cities as well.
04Can my son or daughter book for me from abroad?
Yes. Bookings can be made by senior citizens themselves, by their children living abroad or in another city, or by a family friend or neighbour. Just call or WhatsApp +91 73393 39323.
05How far in advance should I book?
For planned visits like doctor appointments, 24–48 hours is ideal. For emergencies or same-day needs, call our dedicated hotline and we'll do our best based on the availability of a Sakha / Sakhi.
06Can I book the same Sakha / Sakhi every week?
Absolutely. Many seniors prefer building a rapport. If your chosen Sakha / Sakhi is free, you can block recurring slots — for example, every Tuesday morning.
